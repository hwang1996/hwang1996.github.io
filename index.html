Wang Hao (王浩)

I graduated from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) with B.Eng, majoring in Automation.

Currently, I am a first-year PhD student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My supervisors are Miao Chun Yan (principal), Guosheng Lin and Steven Hoi.

From 2018 to 2019, I was a research engineer at Living Analytics Research Centre (LARC), Singapore Management University (SMU). I worked closely with Prof Steven Hoi and Prof Ee-peng Lim on FoodAI project. I started my research journey as an intern in CASIA and Horizon Robotics Inc., Beijing in my undergraduate years, where I really had a great time.

Research Interests
  • Computer Vision, Multimedia, Cross-Modal Retrieval
